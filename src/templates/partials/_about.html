About us

Our company provides professional painters and PA (paint assistants) for Lufthansa Technik Sofia since 2009. Some of our crew members are working on projects of Lufthansa Technik Malta. We also work with Aero Technic, Rose Air and Plane Tech Solutions.

We employ over 100 highly qualified people, who are mainly trained in Bulgaria, Hungary, Romania and in Ireland to deliver a premium quality.

We currently work on 10 location: Bratislava, Toulouse, Lelystad, Kaunas, Maastricht, Paris Le Bourget, Tallinn, Norwich, Malta and Sofia
